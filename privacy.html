Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

1. Overview

iSwing is a badminton community application operated by MiroSports. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account details such as name, email, and profile photo.
  • Profile information you provide in the app.
  • Usage data required to secure and improve the service.

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide sign-in and account features.
  • Enable core app experiences like events and community features.
  • Improve reliability, performance, and product quality.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share information with trusted service providers that support hosting, authentication, and analytics.

5. Data Retention

We keep data only as long as necessary for product operation, legal obligations, and security requirements.

6. Contact
